About Us

Purrfect Match is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping animals find their forever homes. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome cats and dogs in need, and to provide education and resources to pet owners in our community.

Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate animal lovers who are committed to making a difference in the lives of animals. We have a dedicated staff of veterinarians, animal care specialists, and volunteers who work tirelessly to ensure that every animal in our care receives the love and attention they deserve.
